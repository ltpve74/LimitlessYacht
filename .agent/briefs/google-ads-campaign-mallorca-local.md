# Google Ads — Campaign D: "In Mallorca Now" (Geo-Local)
## Manual entry sheet · copy-paste ready · Limitless Yacht Charter

> **Concept:** This campaign targets only people who are **physically present on the island of Mallorca** when they search. These are tourists already on holiday who have spontaneous or last-minute booking intent. The geo-filter does the heavy work — so keywords can be broader and ad copy shifts from aspiration to urgency and proximity.
>
> Build in **Expert Mode** → "Create a campaign without a goal's guidance" → **Search**.

## Geo-targeting — the critical setting

### Step 1 — Add the Mallorca region

In Google Ads → Campaign → **Settings** → **Locations** → **Search for a location**:

```
Search: "Mallorca"
Select: "Majorca (Mallorca), Illes Balears, Spain"
```

### Step 2 — Add a radius as backup (optional but recommended)

Locations → **Advanced search** → **Radius** tab → search `Palma de Mallorca, Spain` → set **50 km** → Add.

### Step 3 — ⚠️ Location option (MOST IMPORTANT SETTING)

After adding locations, click **"Location options"** and set:

```
Target: Presence — People in or regularly in your targeted locations
```

**Do NOT leave this on "Presence or interest" — that would serve ads to people in London
searching for "yacht charter mallorca" and defeat the entire purpose of this campaign.**
