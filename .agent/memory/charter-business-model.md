Limitless has historically been a **day-charter** yacht. As of the 2026 season it has taken its **first multi-day charters**, acquired via **Click&Boat**. The business is NOT a traditional weekly / APA (Advance Provisioning Allowance) charter operation.

**Why it matters:** positioning and pricing should lead with **day charters**, with **multi-day/overnight** framed as a flexible extension ("stay as many days as you like, priced per day") — not week-long bookings. The fixed "1 Week" price was removed from the pricing cards for this reason (it implied a yacht-week model they don't run).

**How to apply:** keep messaging day-first + multi-day flexible; avoid weekly/APA framing. Reviews + availability are fed from the Click&Boat listing (see existing Netlify functions). Relates to [[no-auto-push]] (don't push pricing changes until agreed).
