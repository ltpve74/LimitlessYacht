Suggested Routes

Mallorca's Hidden Coves

01 · Southwest Coast
Es Trenc
White Sands & Turquoise Waters

One of Mallorca's last unspoilt beaches — a sweeping arc of powdery white sand lapped by shallow, crystal-clear water. Anchor just offshore and wade in for a truly secluded escape.

From Club de Mar ~2 h Depth 3–6 m Best for Sunbathing
02 · Southwest Coast
Cala Llamp
Sheltered & Serene

A calm, pine-fringed bay tucked just south of Port d'Andratx. Protected from the prevailing winds, its still green water and relaxed atmosphere make it perfect for a long lunch at anchor.

From Club de Mar ~45 min Depth 4–8 m Best for Snorkelling
03 · Southwest Coast
Portals Vells
Ancient Caves & Clear Depths

Three secluded coves at the tip of the Calvià peninsula, famous for their sea caves carved into the limestone cliffs. The water is a vivid blue-green and ideal for diving and swimming.

From Club de Mar ~1 h Depth 5–10 m Best for Diving & Caves
04 · Northwest Coast
Sa Calobra
Dramatic Gorge & Wild Nature

Accessible only by sea or a legendary mountain road, this dramatic ravine cut through towering limestone cliffs is one of Mallorca's most breathtaking spectacles. A must for any serious itinerary.

From Club de Mar ~2.5 h Depth 6–12 m Best for Hiking & Scenery
