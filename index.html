The Yacht

Your Home on the
Mediterranean

Limitless is a 21-metre Maiora with a flybridge, aft deck and sun pads — space for twelve guests by day, comfortable for seven overnight. Built for the sea and maintained to charter standard.

What truly sets Limitless apart is her dedicated crew. Attentive, experienced and genuinely passionate about the sea, they take care of everything — from organising catering to reserving beach club access — so you can focus entirely on the people you're with and the places you're discovering.

Destinations

Where Limitless Takes You

Every route is shaped around your group — the suggestions below are a starting point. Your crew handles the rest.

01 · Southwest Coast
Portals Vells
Sea Caves & Turquoise Coves

Three secluded coves at the tip of the Calvià peninsula, famous for their sea caves carved into the limestone cliffs. The water is a vivid blue-green — ideal for swimming, snorkelling and a short escape just 35 minutes from the marina.

From Club de Mar ~35 minDepth 4–10 mRound trip ~18 nmDiesel ~300 L
02 · Southwest Coast
El Toro & Illes Malgrats
Marine Reserve & Snorkelling

A protected marine reserve around the Malgrats islets and El Toro headland. Crystal water teeming with fish, easy snorkelling and a relaxed half-day with lunch at anchor.

From Club de Mar ~40 minDepth 5–12 mRound trip ~22 nmDiesel ~350 L
03 · Southwest Coast
Cala Llamp
Sheltered & Serene

A calm, pine-fringed bay tucked just south of Port d’Andratx. Protected from the prevailing winds — perfect for a long lunch at anchor or a peaceful swim.

From Club de Mar ~55 minDepth 4–8 mRound trip ~30 nmDiesel ~450 L

04 · West Coast
Sa Dragonera & Sant Elm
Island Nature Reserve

The uninhabited, dragon-shaped island of Sa Dragonera is a Parc Natural. Snorkel the pristine waters, then lunch in the pretty harbour of Sant Elm.

From Club de Mar ~1 h 10Round trip ~36 nmParc Natural — buoys onlyDiesel ~550 L
05 · South Coast
Cala Pi
Sheltered Cliffs & Fine Sand

A dramatic cove enclosed by towering limestone walls opening onto a sandy beach with shallow turquoise water. Peaceful, photogenic and perfect for swimming and lunch.

From Club de Mar ~1 h 5Depth 3–6 mRound trip ~34 nmDiesel ~550 L
06 · South Coast
Es Trenc
Caribbean-White Sand

One of Mallorca’s last unspoilt beaches — powdery white sand, shallow crystal-clear water. Anchor and wade in for a truly secluded escape.

From Club de Mar ~1 h 30Depth 3–6 mRound trip ~48 nmDiesel ~750 L
07 · South Archipelago
Cabrera National Park
Protected Archipelago

Spain’s first maritime-terrestrial national park. We hold the annual permit — crystal waters, rich wildlife and a true sense of remoteness just 90 minutes from the marina.

From Club de Mar ~1 h 40Round trip ~52 nmPermit held · Buoys onlyDiesel ~800 L
08 · Southeast Coast
Caló des Moro & Cala Figuera
Mallorca’s Most Photographed Cove

The impossibly turquoise inlet of Caló des Moro backed by dramatic cliffs — one of the island’s most iconic spots. Pair with Cala Figuera’s charming fishing harbour for contrast.

From Club de Mar ~2 h 10Depth 4–10 mRound trip ~70 nmDiesel ~1,100 L

09 · Northwest Coast
West Coast Exploration
Sa Foradada, Port Sóller & Cala Tuent

Mallorca’s most dramatic coastline — towering Serra de Tramuntana cliffs, the pierced rock of Sa Foradada, the harbour town of Port Sóller and the secluded pebble beach of Cala Tuent. 2–4 nights.

From Club de Mar ~2 h 55Round trip ~94 nmAnchorages & mooringsDiesel ~1,450 L
10 · Full Island
Mallorca Circumnavigation
Complete 200 nm Loop

The definitive Mallorca voyage — all 200 nm of coastline, from the wild west cliffs and Cap de Formentor to the calm eastern bays and back. 5–7 flexible days at anchor or in harbour towns.

Cruising time ~12 h 30Total ~200 nm5–7 days recommendedDiesel est. ~3,150 L
11 · Balearics Crossing
Ibiza & Formentera
White Isle & Turquoise Spits

A ~70 nm open-sea crossing to Ibiza’s vibrant harbours and hidden coves, with the option to push on to Formentera’s Ses Illetes — the Caribbean of the Med. 2–4 nights.

From Club de Mar ~4 h 25Round trip ~140 nmMarina or anchorageDiesel ~2,200 L
12 · Balearics Crossing
Menorca Crossing
Wild Coves & Ancient Shores

~60 nm northeast to Menorca — the most unspoilt Balearic. Anchor in Cala Macarella and Cala Mitjana, moor in Mahón’s vast natural harbour, discover quiet fishing towns. 2–5 nights.

From Club de Mar ~3 h 45Round trip ~120 nmCoves & natural harbourDiesel ~1,900 L
